Oxtail & Beans Rice

Description

Oxtail and beans rice is a hearty dish made with tender oxtail meat, cooked until it falls off the bone, served over a bed of fluffy rice and beans. The dish is often seasoned with spices and herbs, making it a flavorful and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Oxtail
  • Beans (Red Beans)
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell Peppers
  • Chili Peppers
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Maggi

Steps

  1. Clean the oxtail and season it with salt, Maggi, and spices.
  2. In a pot, heat vegetable oil and saut√© onions until translucent.
  3. Add the oxtail and brown it on all sides.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, bell peppers, and chili peppers to the pot.
  5. Cover and let it simmer until the oxtail is tender.
  6. In a separate pot, cook the beans until soft.
  7. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  8. Serve the oxtail over a bed of rice and beans.
  9. Garnish with additional vegetables if desired.

Optional

Some people like to add a sauce made from tomatoes, onions, and chili peppers to enhance the flavor of the dish.
