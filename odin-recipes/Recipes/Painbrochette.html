Pain Brochette

Description

Pain brochette is a popular street food in West Africa, particularly in CÃ´te d'Ivoire. It consists of skewered and grilled meat, often served with a spicy sauce and accompanied by bread. The dish is known for its smoky flavor and is typically enjoyed as a quick snack or meal.

Ingredients

  • Baguette or French bread
  • Meat (beef, chicken, or fish)
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Chili peppers
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Maggi seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into small pieces and marinate with salt, pepper, Maggi seasoning, and spices.
  2. Skewer the marinated meat along with pieces of onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes.
  3. Grill the skewers over an open flame or on a grill until cooked through.
  4. Slice the baguette or French bread and toast it lightly.
  5. Serve the grilled skewers on the toasted bread, garnished with sliced chili peppers and a spicy sauce if desired.
  6. Enjoy your delicious pain brochette!

Optional

Some people like to add a spicy sauce made from tomatoes, onions, and chili peppers to enhance the flavor of the dish.
